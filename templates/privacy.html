Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. What We Collect

When you use YourMeet, we collect:

  • Telegram data: Your Telegram user ID, first name, and language code (provided by Telegram)
  • Profile data: Name, age, gender, city, bio, interests, phone number, social handle
  • Photos: Stored via Telegram's servers using file IDs
  • Activity data: Likes, matches, swipes, chat sessions
  • Location: City name and approximate coordinates (for map discovery)

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To show your profile to potential matches
  • To send you match and chat notifications via Telegram bot
  • To provide map-based discovery features
  • To process premium payments via Telegram Stars
  • To enforce our Terms of Service

3. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your data. Your data is shared only:

  • With other users — only the profile info you choose to display
  • With Telegram — as required to operate the bot and mini app
  • With Turso (database hosting) — encrypted storage

4. Photos

Your photos are stored on Telegram's servers. We store only the Telegram file ID, not the actual image file. Photos are displayed only within the app.

5. Chat Sessions

Bot chat messages are forwarded in real-time and are NOT stored. Once a chat session ends, no message history is retained.

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all your data is permanently removed including likes, matches, and profile information.

7. Your Rights

  • Access your data via the profile page
  • Edit your data via the profile edit feature
  • Delete all your data via "Delete Account"

8. Security

We use HMAC-SHA256 to verify Telegram identity on every session. JWT tokens are used for authentication. All connections are over HTTPS.

9. Children

YourMeet is strictly for users 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If we discover a minor has registered, the account will be immediately terminated.

10. Contact
